Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about agricultural subsidies

Our database is updated weekly with the latest government schemes from central and state agricultural departments. We monitor official gazettes and ministry announcements to ensure you have access to the most current programs.

Our subsidy finder is completely free to use. AgroTech makes money through premium services like document preparation and application filing assistance, but discovering available subsidies will always be free for farmers.

Requirements vary by scheme but typically include: land records (7/12 extract), Aadhaar card, bank details, caste certificate (if applicable), and sometimes photos of your farm. For equipment subsidies, you may need dealer quotations. We provide a customized checklist for each subsidy you qualify for.

Processing times vary by scheme but typically range from 30-90 days after submitting a complete application. Some subsidies are paid upfront (like training programs), while others reimburse after purchase (like equipment subsidies). We track your application status and follow up with authorities if delays occur.
